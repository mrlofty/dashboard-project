# CRM Tier/Type Redesign

*Captured: 2026-02-14 (Valentine's Day session v2)*

## Key Insight: Tier ≠ Frequency

- **Tier** = importance/closeness (how much they matter)
- **Frequency** = how often you interact (actual contact cadence)

These are independent:
- Haydon: Core tier, low frequency (distance)
- Work colleague: High frequency, maybe lower tier

## Proposed Tier Structure

```
📇 Contact (just details, no relationship)
👋 Acquaintance (met, but not maintained)
───────────────────────────────────────────
🔵 Peripheral (occasional contact)
🟢 Regular (normal friend/colleague)
🟡 Close (good friends)
🟣 Core (inner circle)
```

The line matters:
- **Above**: People I actually invest in (relationships)
- **Below**: People in my phone (contacts)

## Import Flow

Everything comes in as Contact/Acquaintance → promote ones that are real relationships.

## Type (How You Know Them)

Separate from tier — describes the *nature* of the relationship:
- **Personal** (friends, family)
- **Professional** (work, industry)
- **Romantic** (dating, partners, exes)
- **Community** (clubs, scenes, online)

Examples:
- Core + Romantic = current partner
- Close + Professional = work friend who became real friend
- Peripheral + Community = someone from MCC you've met twice

## Signal Strength Widget 📶

Track relationship health:
- **Last contacted**: date of last interaction
- **Preferred cadence**: weekly, monthly, quarterly
- **Signal strength**: are you maintaining at expected level?
- **Alerts**: "Haven't talked to [Core person] in 3 weeks"
